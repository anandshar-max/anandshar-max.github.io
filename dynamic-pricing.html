Dynamic Pricing

Overview

Dynamic Pricing continuously adjusts room rates based on demand signals, competitor pricing, and local events. Our AI-driven engine recommends optimal prices to maximize RevPAR and occupancy.

Impact So Far

  • 12% uplift in average RevPAR across our top 20 properties
  • 8% increase in occupancy during shoulder periods
  • Reduced manual rate overrides by 90%

Metrics Tracked

  • RevPAR (Revenue per Available Room)
  • ADR (Average Daily Rate)
  • Occupancy Rate
  • Yield (Revenue vs. Potential Revenue)
